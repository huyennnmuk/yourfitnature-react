7-Day Bloating Reset Tracker

Introduction: Welcome to your 7-Day Bloating Reset! This tracker is designed to help you pinpoint the triggers behind your bloating. For the next seven days, diligently log your meals, symptoms, and daily habits. This simple act of tracking is the most powerful first step you can take toward a calmer, more comfortable gut. Note any patterns you see emerging.

Day 2

Date: _______________

Tip of the day: Try a short, 5-10 minute walk after your largest meal. Gentle movement can stimulate digestion and help move gas through your system.

Day 3

Date: _______________

Tip of the day: Avoid drinking carbonated beverages today, including sparkling water. The fizz can introduce excess gas into your digestive system.

Day 4

Date: _______________

Tip of the day: Add a teaspoon of ginger to your tea or a meal. Ginger is well-known for its ability to soothe the digestive tract and relieve nausea.

Day 5

Date: _______________

Tip of the day: Focus on eating in a relaxed state. Avoid eating while stressed, working, or watching TV. Your digestive system works best when your body is calm.

Day 6

Date: _______________

Tip of the day: Consider a low-FODMAP snack today, like a handful of blueberries or a kiwi. FODMAPs are types of carbohydrates that can be difficult for some people to digest.

Day 7

Date: _______________

Tip of the day: Reflect on your notes from the week. Do you see any connections between a specific food and your bloating symptoms? This is your first clue!
