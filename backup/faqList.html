How long does implementation take?

Most providers are up and running within 4-6 weeks. We do a thorough data and system health check, including submitting test claims and checking access, before going live to ensure a smooth transition from your current platform.

Which systems do you integrate with?

We integrate with most Electronic Health Record (EHR) and Practice Management Software (PMS) systems. Our team will work with you to ensure a seamless connection with your existing systems, and we are able to build new integrations within the same implementation timelines as any standard onboarding.

How do statements and payments work?

Our client portal provides digital statements with 2-click payments, including Apple Pay and Google Pay options, as well as QR codes from printed statements. We also provide flexible payment plans and a secure portal for clients.
